BUDDYBOY (ESCAPE FROM WORK)

Run horizontally from 09:00 to 17:00. You start with 10 energy, each hit removes 1.

Enemies: bad bosses, tripping coworkers, endless video calls, incoming emails, production war rooms, hacker attacks, budgets and clients.

Final stage: the giant BIG BOSS appears and you must defeat it to complete the shift.

Eliminate enemies by shooting MAIL or MEETINGS: every takedown gives points.
